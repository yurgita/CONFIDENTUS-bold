About us

CONFIDENTUS is a privately owned and independent Lithuanian investment banking company established in 2009.
CONFIDENTUS partners are successful practitioners that have more than 18 years of experience in corporate finance and banking. We provide a broad array of high-quality investment banking services.
CONFIDENTUS is proud member of M&A WORLDWIDE, the global network of M&A companies. We also have cooperational partners in Latvia, Estonia, Scandinavia, Poland and Germany, who deliver local market knowledge to cross-border deals. For the secon year in a row CONFIDENTUS got ACQ Global Award: Corporate finance boutique and M&A advisor of the year 2017 in Lithuania.
