About
Karolis Janulis is a drone inspired aerial photographer from Klaipėda, Lithuania. The breakthrough in Karolis as a photographer carrier happened in 2015 when he has started implementing his photography visions from the sky with the help of a drone. In the same year his photography has been featured by cnn, bbc, dailymail, american photo mag, abc, rotor drone, the telegraph, the guardian, wired, f11 and many other worldwide media. In 2016 he has been shortlisted in the Sony World Photography Awards, people category.
