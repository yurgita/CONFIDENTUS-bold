M&A WORLDWIDE - The Network for Mergers and Acquisitions

CONFIDENTUS is a proud member of M&A WORLDWIDE, the global network of M&A companies.

Currently M&A WORLDWIDE includes 43 member companies in 45 countries of the world, having over 360 specialists in the most economically significant regions of the world. At the end of 2017, the network registered and completed 339 successful deals that brought a total value of over USD 2.9 billion.

Through our M&A WORLDWIDE network, which covers six continents, we can access industry expertise, a large number of strategic investors, transaction targets and financial partners to ensure a successful transaction for our client.

The network allows for inter-cultural cooperation that is a key success-factor to almost all cross-border transactions.
