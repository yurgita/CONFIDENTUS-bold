M&A Worldwide - The Network for Mergers and Acquisitions

CONFIDENTUS is proud member of M&A Worldwide, the global network of M&A companies.

M&A worldwide now comprises of 40 member companies in 38 countries of the world, and, with over 200 specialists in the most economically significant regions of the world. The members of the organization reported a total of 641 transactions with a total volume of 3,1 billion Euro for the year 2016.

Through our M&A Worldwide network, covering six continents, we provide access to industry expertise, a large number of strategic buyers, transaction targets and financial partners to ensure a successful transaction.

The network allows for inter-cultural cooperation that is a key success-factor to almost all cross-border transactions.
